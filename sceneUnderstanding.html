What is Scene Understanding

Scene Understanding focuses on interpreting and concentrating context of a scene through both preceptive and analytical process that mimic the way the human mind understands. It tries to recreate the human visual preceptive digitally through different algorithms and machine learning in order to interpret, analyze, and understand different digital images or different video frames with both precision and speed.
